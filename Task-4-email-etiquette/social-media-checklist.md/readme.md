# 📱 Responsible Social Media Use – College Student Checklist

This checklist outlines essential **Do’s and Don’ts** to help college students maintain a responsible, safe, and positive presence on social media.

---

## ✅ Do’s

* **Think before you post** – Consider how your content may affect your reputation now and in the future.
* **Protect your privacy** – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings to control who sees your content.
* **Be respectful and kind** – Engage in positive conversations and respect differing opinions.
* **Verify information before sharing** – Avoid spreading misinformation by checking reliable sources.
* **Build a positive digital footprint** – Share content that reflects your skills, achievements, and interests.

---

## ❌ Don’ts

* **Don’t overshare personal information** – Avoid posting sensitive details like your address, phone number, or financial info.
* **Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling** – Harmful behavior online can have serious consequences.
* **Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content** – It can impact your academic and career opportunities.
* **Don’t believe everything you see online** – Be critical of content and avoid falling for fake news or scams.
* **Don’t let social media affect your productivity** – Set limits to avoid excessive usage that interferes with studies.

---

## 📌 Summary

Using social media responsibly helps you stay safe, maintain a good reputation, and make the most of your college experience. Always aim to create a **positive, respectful, and mindful online presence**.
